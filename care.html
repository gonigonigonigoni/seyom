Hamster Care Tips

Learn how to take proper care of your hamster with our essential care tips.

  • Provide a spacious and clean cage.
  • Offer a balanced diet with fresh fruits and vegetables.
  • Ensure fresh water is always available.
  • Give your hamster plenty of exercise with wheels and toys.
  • Handle your hamster gently and regularly to build trust.
