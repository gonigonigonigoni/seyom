Hamster Health Issues

Common Cold

Hamsters can catch colds. Keep their environment warm and clean to prevent this.

Wet Tail

Wet tail is a serious condition. If your hamster has diarrhea, consult a vet immediately.

Teeth Problems

Hamster teeth grow continuously. Provide chew toys to help them wear down their teeth.
