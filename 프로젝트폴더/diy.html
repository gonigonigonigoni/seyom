DIY Hamster Toys

Cardboard Tube Tunnel

Use cardboard tubes from paper towels or toilet paper to create fun tunnels for your hamster to explore.

Homemade Hamster Wheel

Create a homemade hamster wheel using household items like a plastic bowl and wire coat hanger.

Chew Toys

Make chew toys using untreated wood, popsicle sticks, or woven straw.
